Welcome to Tooth n Care

Tooth n Care is a modern dental clinic in East Maitland offering a full range of dental services for you and your family, all under one roof, in a clean, calm and comfortable setting.

Our focus is simple: you. We take the time to understand what's going on, explain your options clearly, and build a plan that suits your needs, your goals and your budget. No jargon, no pressure: just honest, considered care.

Open six days a week, including Saturdays 9am–4pm.

New patients, walk-ins and emergencies welcome.

A gentle, unhurried approach for anxious patients.

Modern technology, including intraoral cameras and single-visit CEREC crowns.

Interest-free payment plans and all health funds welcome, with on-the-spot HICAPS claiming.

Offers

New-patient offer

New to Tooth n Care? Get your smile off to a healthy start. Your first visit covers a comprehensive examination, personalised treatment plan, X-rays, a scale and clean, and a fluoride treatment.

In a private health fund? We'll waive the gap on these items where your fund rebates at least $199, so there's nothing to pay out of pocket. No health cover? It's a flat $199. For new patients only; a $50 booking deposit applies and comes off the cost of your visit.

FAQ

Questions? We've got answers.

All health funds are welcome, and we're set up with HICAPS, so if you have private dental cover you can claim on the spot and only pay any gap. Bring your health fund card to your visit.

Your first visit covers a comprehensive examination, personalised treatment plan, X-rays, a scale and clean, and a fluoride treatment. We'll waive the gap where your fund rebates at least $199, or it's a flat $199 without cover. For new patients only; a $50 booking deposit applies and comes off the cost of your visit.

Many children are eligible for the government's Child Dental Benefits Schedule: a benefit towards basic dental services of up to $1,158 over two consecutive calendar years. Ask our team and we'll help you check.

If you're in pain or you've had an accident, call us on 02 4933 4667. We'll do our best to see you quickly. We welcome emergencies and walk-ins, and we're open Saturdays too.

If you feel anxious, tell us. We'll take things at your pace, explain each step, and use gentle techniques and comfort options such as in-chair entertainment to help you relax.

Yes, interest-free payment plans are available through providers such as Humm (National Dental Plan) and DentiCare. Lending criteria, fees and terms and conditions apply. See our Fees & Payment Plans page for details.
